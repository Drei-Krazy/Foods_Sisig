Sizzling Pork Sisig

Let's put a spin on a traditional favorite! An all-time beloved classic of crispy chicharon and grilled meats served in a creamy, tangy mayo sauce made extra rich with egg for a mouthwatering pork appetizer. Learn how to make Sizzling Pork Sisig here.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork belly, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 red chili peppers, minced
  • 1 green chili pepper, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Saute: In a hot pan, saute minced pork belly until crispy.
  2. Add Aromatics: Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and chili peppers. Saut√© until aromatic.
  3. Season: Mix in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Finish: Add mayonnaise and egg. Stir until well combined and continue cooking until the edges are crispy.
  5. Serve: Serve hot and sizzling! Garnish with additional chili peppers if desired.
